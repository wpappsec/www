## WPAPPSEC Checklist

We will also be publishing helpful guides on these subjects and connecting suppliers who help in these areas. 

Think of this as a conversation starter to have with yourself or your team and suppliers. Most of these questions will probaly resutl in more questions and some interesting conversations. 

- Do we know our threat profiles?
- Are we using 2FA or MFA?
- Is our email secure? 
- Are our devices secure?
- Is our network secure?
- Are our passwords managed securely?
- Are we sending files securely?
- Is our Domain Registrar account secure?
- Is our DNS records server / account secure?
- Are we using somthing like a CDN for DDoS protection?
- Are we using a WAF?
- Do we have backups?
- Have we prepared for a disaster recovery? 
- Is our server and its software patched and managed? 
- Do we have access and other logs? 
- Do we have a content security policy?
- Are we manage our CMS users?
- Are we managing our CMS core and plugin updates?
- Are we forcing strong passwords?
- Are we limiting logins?
- Are we blocking or allowing IPs? 
- Are we managing our code repoositories?
- Are we managing our aplication and intergrations keys and passwords? 
- Are we managing our dependencies? 
- Are we securing our testing and alnaytical code injection tools such as GTM VWO? 
- Are we scanning our application? 
- Do we have alerting and monitoring? 
- Is our content exposing personal or sensitive information?
- Are we securing 3rd Party content?
- Are we auding pur code?
