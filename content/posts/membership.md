## WPAPPSEC Membership

A member can be WPAPPSEC AA compliant if they have raised all AA issues into a specification, design, product backlog or into a project plan etc. 

So members can claim to be WPAPPSEC compliant, or specify their compliance by saying for example they are WPAPPSEC AA compliant.
